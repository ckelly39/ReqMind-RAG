FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS SPECIFICATION
E-Commerce Platform

1. USER AUTHENTICATION AND AUTHORIZATION

REQ-001: User Registration
The system shall allow new users to register by providing email, password, and basic profile information.
Priority: High
Status: Approved

REQ-002: User Login
The system shall authenticate users using email and password credentials.
The system shall support multi-factor authentication (MFA) as an optional security feature.
Priority: High
Status: Approved

REQ-003: Password Recovery
The system shall provide a password recovery mechanism via email verification.
Users shall be able to reset their password using a time-limited token sent to their registered email.
Priority: Medium
Status: Approved

2. PRODUCT CATALOG MANAGEMENT

REQ-004: Product Browsing
The system shall allow users to browse products by category, brand, and price range.
The system shall display product images, descriptions, prices, and availability status.
Priority: High
Status: Approved

REQ-005: Product Search
The system shall provide a search functionality with autocomplete suggestions.
The system shall support filtering and sorting of search results.
Priority: High
Status: Approved

REQ-006: Product Details
The system shall display detailed product information including specifications, reviews, and ratings.
The system shall show related products and recommendations.
Priority: Medium
Status: Approved

3. SHOPPING CART AND CHECKOUT

REQ-007: Shopping Cart
The system shall allow users to add, remove, and update product quantities in their shopping cart.
The cart shall persist across user sessions.
Priority: High
Status: Approved

REQ-008: Checkout Process
The system shall guide users through a multi-step checkout process.
The system shall calculate total price including taxes and shipping costs.
Priority: High
Status: Approved

REQ-009: Payment Processing
The system shall support multiple payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets.
The system shall securely process payment transactions using PCI-DSS compliant methods.
Priority: Critical
Status: Approved

4. ORDER MANAGEMENT

REQ-010: Order Placement
The system shall generate a unique order ID for each successful transaction.
The system shall send order confirmation emails to customers.
Priority: High
Status: Approved

REQ-011: Order Tracking
The system shall allow users to track their order status in real-time.
The system shall provide estimated delivery dates.
Priority: Medium
Status: Approved

REQ-012: Order History
The system shall maintain a complete history of user orders.
Users shall be able to view past orders and reorder items.
Priority: Medium
Status: Approved

5. INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

REQ-013: Stock Management
The system shall track product inventory levels in real-time.
The system shall prevent overselling by checking availability before order confirmation.
Priority: High
Status: Approved

REQ-014: Low Stock Alerts
The system shall generate alerts when product inventory falls below defined thresholds.
Priority: Medium
Status: Under Review

6. CUSTOMER SUPPORT

REQ-015: Customer Reviews
The system shall allow verified purchasers to submit product reviews and ratings.
The system shall display average ratings and review counts on product pages.
Priority: Medium
Status: Approved

REQ-016: Customer Service Contact
The system shall provide multiple contact channels including email, chat, and phone support.
The system shall include a FAQ section for common queries.
Priority: Medium
Status: Approved
